illustration, I wanted to give the sense
that Link was confronting the world.
My first sketch portrayed Link
looking out over Hyrule with bis back
to the viewer. I then experimented
with several other compositions that
more prominently featured Link, but
eventually settled on the direction of my
initial idea. I felt that the world itself, not
just the characters, had a leading role in
this game.
I also wanted to convey that this was
the beginning of a new Zelda, so I drew
the sky at daybreak.
